ABOUT

Jeff Ryan is a filmmaker hailing from the greatest state in America: New Jersey. Despite his lofty dreams of becoming a successful filmmaker, he, like so many others on this circus ride, found himself facing the sweet smell of rejection on a daily basis. Earlier this year, Jeff found himself falling to his knees and crying into an unwashed pillow, after receiving his ninth rejection from SXSW. It was then that Jeff decided to create a festival of his own. After a record number of submissions in its inaugural year, Jeff feels confident that mortgaging his home and personal assets to make this dream a reality was a smart decision. Time will tell, but Jeff believes he can double his submission count by 2026 if the stars continue to align.
